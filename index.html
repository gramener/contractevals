What risks do contracts carry?

Legal teams need to ensure that their publishing contracts are correctly reviewed and compliant with legal guidelines, ensuring proper rights assignment, copyright transfers, and adherence to contractual obligations.

This process is critical for protecting intellectual property rights, avoiding legal disputes, and ensuring proper licensing and usage of content under its agreements.

In this exercise, paralegals reviewed hundreds of contracts to:

  1. Verify completeness and signatures of all required parties.
  2. Assess the type and scope of rights granted to the publisher.
  3. Confirm if the publisher has received full copyrigh and necessary rights.
  4. Review amendments for risks, representations, warranties, and indemnities.
  5. Based on the above, provide a licensing recommendation along with a rationale.
